I AM MAXINE DOMINIQUE

E. ENRIQUEZ

Maxi

I am Maxine Dominique E. Enriquez, a second-year Multimedia Arts
student at FEU Institute of Technology. Born on December 23, 2004,
I am currently 19 years old. I identify myself as a lesbian, I take pride
in being a vocal advocate for the rights of the LGBTQIA+ community
and use my voice for women's and children's rights. Photography and
graphic design are some of my cherished hobbies and are my
favorite fields in multimedia arts.
